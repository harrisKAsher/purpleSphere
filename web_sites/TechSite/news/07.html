iPhone:
One of the most revolutionary things we have today, it combines a Music Player and a Cell Phone into a revolutionary device which can play Music, Make Phone Calls, Text, Play Games, Search the Internet, Talk on Social Networks, built in GPS, and the list goes on and on. It allows people to have better access to everything and all in one place, all of the past technologies in a small gadget less than a pound and fits in the palm of your hand.
